[Description of the illustration manage_workspace.png]

This images shows the Manage Workspaces dialog while signed in as USER1. While signed in as USER1, you can view the default workspace owner by USER1, and all other workspaces that are shared with USER1. In this screenshot, USER1 selects USER1 Workspace and clicks Permission.
